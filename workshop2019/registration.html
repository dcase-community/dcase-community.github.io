Workshop Registration

Registration

The DCASE2019 Workshop will take place on Friday, 25th October, and Saturday, 26th October, at New York University's Tandon School of Engineering in Brooklyn, NY, USA.

Registration for DCASE is now closed

 

Authors: At least one author for each paper must register by 6th September 2019.

Submitting a paper or a contribution to the challenge is not a condition for participation in the workshop: Everyone can attend.

 

The fees are as follows:

Participant Early Registration
(by 6th September)		 Regular Registration
(by 1st October)
Standard $250 $325
Student* $150 $225
  • For Registration at the Student rate, please send a letter confirming your student status from your supervisor or institution via email to: dcase.workshop@gmail.com

 

The registration fee includes entrance to the workshop, coffee/tea breaks, lunch (daily) and the Welcome Reception on Friday 25th October.

If you need to a visa letter before registration opens, please e-mail dcase.workshop@gmail.com.
