Location

 

The 2019 edition of the DCASE workshop will take place at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering in New York, NY, USA.

The NYU Tandon School of Engineering, formerly known as the Brooklyn Collegiate and Polytechnic Institute, was founded in 1854. It merged with New York University (NYU) in 2014.

The scientific program of DCASE 2019, including keynote sessions, oral sessions, and poster sessions, will happen in Pfizer Auditorium, at the following address: Bern Dibner Library of Science and Technology 5, MetroTech Center, Brooklyn, NY 11245.

MetroTech Center is a a business and educational center in the neighborhood of downtown Brooklyn. The Bern Dibner Library overlooks the MetroTech Commons, a large open plaza which regularly hosts concerts, fitness classes, tournaments, and holiday celebrations.
