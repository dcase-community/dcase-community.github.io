Call for Sponsorship - DCASE Workshop 2023 (Tampere, Finland)

Thu 20 April 2023, Annamaria Mesaros
workshop2023

We would like to extend an invitation for you to become a sponsor of the 8th Workshop on Detection and Classification of Acoustic Scenes and Events, DCASE 2023, which will be held in Tampere, Finland September 21-22 (http://dcase.community/workshop2023/). The workshop aims to provide a venue for researchers (in both academia and industry) working on computational analysis of sound events and scene analysis to present and discuss their results.

We expect at least 150 on-site participants. Keynote and oral presentations will be broadcasted, potentially reaching a larger audience.

Sponsoring DCASE Workshop 2023 will give you a high visibility in the field and an opportunity to join this dynamic community. For sponsoring DCASE Workshop 2023 we offer four levels of sponsorship: Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum. You will find a detailed description of the benefits for each level below.

Bronze Sponsorship (1000€)

  • Company logo with hyperlink at the bottom of the list of sponsors in the workshop’s website front page
  • Small company logo at the bottom of the sponsors banner on the holding slide shown at the start of each DCASE oral presentation
  • Small company logo at the bottom of the sponsors page of the workshop PDF proceedings

Silver Sponsorship (2000€)

  • 2 free registrations to the workshop
  • Company logo with hyperlink at the middle of the list of sponsors in the workshop’s website front page
  • Company logo at the middle of the sponsors banner on the holding slide shown at the start of each DCASE oral presentation
  • Company logo at the middle of the sponsors page of the workshop PDF proceedings

Gold Sponsorship (3000€)

  • 3 free registrations to the workshop
  • Company logo with hyperlink at the middle of the list of sponsors in the workshop’s website front page
  • Company logo at the middle of the sponsors banner on the holding slide shown at the start of each
  • Company logo at the middle of the sponsors page of the workshop PDF proceedings
  • One poster slot in the poster/coffee area to showcase company activity

Platinum Sponsorship (5000€)

  • 4 free registrations to the workshop
  • Company logo with hyperlink at the top of the list of sponsors in the workshop’s website front page
  • Large company logo at the top of the sponsors banner on the holding slide shown at the start of each DCASE oral presentation
  • Large company logo at the top of the sponsors page of the workshop PDF proceedings
  • One booth in the poster/coffee area to showcase company activity
  • One poster/banner at the reception
